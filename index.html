Our Origin Story

The Komal Memorial was born out of a profound loss, but fueled by a beautiful legacy. Komal was a rare soul who saw the world through the lens of compassion. To her, no animal was "just a stray"—every living being deserved dignity, a full bowl of food, and a safe place to rest.

When we lost Komal, we didn't just lose a friend and a colleague; the animal world lost one of its fiercest advocates. We established the Komal Memorial - Friends of Animal Rescue (F.A.R.) to ensure that her mission didn't end with her. We believe that by continuing her work, her heart keeps beating in every life we save.

A Rare Soul, A Gentle Heart

Some people leave behind more than memories—they leave behind kindness that continues to ripple through the world. Komal was one of those rare souls: gentle, selfless, and full of love for every living being.

As a valued member of our organization, she touched many lives, but her deepest passion was reserved for the voiceless. We still remember her dedication to the "10:10:10 initiative" in 2023, where she tirelessly raised awareness for stray animals. Her compassion wasn't just an act of kindness; it was her way of healing the world.

Although she is gone from our sight, she is never from our hearts. Through every life saved by this fund, her heart will keep beating in the world she worked to make kinder.

Where It All Started

To understand the mission of this memorial fund, we look back to Komal’s own passionate words from January 2023.

"We cannot even feel the pain of the mothers whose babies have got injured..."

"In some cases, they mostly suffer and die due to their disease or infection..."

"Let's be a reason for their joy."

— Komal, Jan 31st, 2023
